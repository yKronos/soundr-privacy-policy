Privacy Policy for Soundr

Last updated: January 2026

Soundr (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application, Soundr, handles user information.

Soundr is an offline music player application designed to allow users to play audio files stored locally on their device. We do not collect, store, or share personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Soundr does not collect any personal data.

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, or usage analytics.

2. Permissions Used

Soundr may request access to your device’s media or storage solely for the purpose of detecting and playing music files stored locally on your device.

Soundr does not upload, copy, or share your files. All access remains local to your device.

3. Data Sharing

Soundr does not share any data with third parties, advertisers, analytics providers, or external services.

4. Third-Party Services

Soundr does not use third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or cloud services.

5. Data Security

Because Soundr does not collect or store personal data, no personal information is processed or stored by us.

6. Children’s Privacy

Soundr does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect any data, it complies with children’s privacy protection standards.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date.
